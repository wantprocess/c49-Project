Camel Facts You Should Know!

  • There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  • Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can’t eat.

Facts About Chameleons You Should Know!

  • The eyes of chameleons are located on the backs of their heads.
  • Chameleons come in a variety of sizes and shapes.
  • The tongues of chameleons are very strong.

You Should Know These Pigeon Facts!

  • Pigeons are extremely intelligent and complicated creatures.
  • Pigeons are known for their exceptional navigational skills.
  • Pigeons are gregarious creatures.

These Teddy Bear Facts Are Must-Knows!

  • Teddy bears were named after Teddy Roosevelt, who declined to shoot a bear cub on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The term "teddy bear" was first used in 1906, according to the Oxford English Dictionary.
  • An arctophile is the official term for a teddy bear collector.

These Panda Facts Shouldn't Be Missed!

  • The term "giant panda" is merely a nickname for a panda; they are not in any way larger than ordinary pandas.
  • Pandas eat for 12-14 hours a day, every day!
  • Adult pandas can weigh up to 45 kg (100 pounds).
